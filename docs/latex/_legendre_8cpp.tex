\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Computes the coefficient matrices of Legendre polynomials and their derivatives. 

This function computes the coefficient matrices of Legendre polynomials {\ttfamily pnm} and their derivatives {\ttfamily dpnm} up to a given order {\ttfamily n} and degree {\ttfamily m}, evaluated at angle {\ttfamily fi}.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em n} & Maximum order of the Legendre polynomials. \\
\hline
{\em m} & Maximum degree of the Legendre polynomials. \\
\hline
{\em fi} & Angle in radians at which the Legendre polynomials are evaluated. \\
\hline
{\em pnm} & Reference to a matrix to store the coefficients of Legendre polynomials. \\
\hline
{\em dpnm} & Reference to a matrix to store the coefficients of derivatives of Legendre polynomials. \\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
