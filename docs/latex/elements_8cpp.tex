\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Computes the osculating Keplerian elements from the satellite state vector for elliptic orbits. 

This function computes the osculating Keplerian elements (semilatus rectum, semimajor axis, eccentricity, inclination, longitude of the ascending node, argument of pericenter, and mean anomaly) from the satellite state vector for elliptic orbits.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em y} & State vector (x, y, z, vx, vy, vz) in meters and meters per second.\\
\hline
{\em p} & Semilatus rectum in meters. \\
\hline
{\em a} & Semimajor axis in meters. \\
\hline
{\em e} & Eccentricity. \\
\hline
{\em i} & Inclination in radians. \\
\hline
{\em Omega} & Longitude of the ascending node in radians. \\
\hline
{\em omega} & Argument of pericenter in radians. \\
\hline
{\em M} & Mean anomaly in radians.\\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
