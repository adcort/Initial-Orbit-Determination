\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Computation of the equation of the equinoxes. 

The equation of the equinoxes $d\psi\cos(\epsilon)$ is the right ascension of the mean equinox referred to the true equator and equinox and is equal to the difference between apparent and mean sidereal time.
